About DecorAR

Transform Your Room Design Experience

DecorAR is an innovative room decoration planner that combines the power of 3D visualization with intuitive drag-and-drop functionality. Our mission is to make interior design accessible to everyone, regardless of technical expertise.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, DecorAR was born from the frustration of traditional interior design tools that were either too complex or too limited. We wanted to create a tool that was powerful enough for professionals yet simple enough for homeowners to use.

How It Works

1

Upload Your Room

Start by uploading a photo of your room or use our blank canvas.

2

Drag & Drop Furniture

Select from our library of 3D furniture models and place them in your room.

3

Customize & Arrange

Rotate, scale, and position items to create your perfect layout.

4

Save & Share

Save your design and share it with friends or contractors.

Our Technology

DecorAR is built using modern web technologies including:

  • Three.js - For powerful 3D rendering
  • React - For a responsive and interactive UI
  • HTML5 Drag & Drop - For intuitive furniture placement
  • WebGL - For hardware-accelerated graphics
